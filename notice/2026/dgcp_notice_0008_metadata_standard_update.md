## Notice

Effective from this notice, all DGCP™ global publications shall adopt the following metadata standard:

* Project: MaMeeFarm™
* Role: System Architect
* Location: Earth System

This update supersedes previous metadata variants, including:

* Project: MaMeeFarm™ Global System Observation
* Role: Global Standard Setter
* Location: MaMeeFarm (Primary DGCP Site)

Historical publications remain unchanged as part of the immutable archive and continue to reflect the metadata standard that was in effect at the time of their original publication.

This notice applies only to global publications.

Operational field records, including Daily Logs, Proofs, DataUnits, NFT records, and other on-site observations, shall continue using:

* Location: MaMeeFarm (Primary DGCP Site)

This notice establishes the official metadata standard for future DGCP™ global publications while preserving archival integrity under the append-only governance model.

## Effective Date

This metadata standard becomes effective on 2026-07-02 (Asia/Bangkok).

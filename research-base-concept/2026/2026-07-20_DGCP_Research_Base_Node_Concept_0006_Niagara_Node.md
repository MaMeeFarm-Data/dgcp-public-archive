# DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0006

## Niagara Node

### Great Lakes Water Systems Research Base

**Record ID:** RNC-000006

---

## Concept Statement

> Observe the flow.  
> Understand the system.  
> Respect the balance.  
> Build for the future.

DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0006 explores a long-term research base inspired by the Niagara Falls region and the Great Lakes freshwater system.

The concept investigates the relationships between freshwater resources, renewable energy, ecosystems, biodiversity, climate, transboundary cooperation, and sustainable regional development within one integrated research environment.

## Architectural Concept

The architectural language emphasizes openness, sustainability and continuous connection with water.

Design principles include:

- respect the water
- open connection to nature
- durable four-season architecture
- local materials
- sustainable operation
- collaborative research spaces

The architecture supports observation while remaining integrated with the surrounding landscape.

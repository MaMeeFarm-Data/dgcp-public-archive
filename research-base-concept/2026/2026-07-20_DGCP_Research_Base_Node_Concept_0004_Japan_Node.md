# DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0004

## Japan Node

### Hokkaido Research Base

**Record ID:** RNC-000004

---

## Concept Statement

> Observe nature.  
> Understand seasonal systems.  
> Preserve evidence.  
> Design for resilience.

DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0004 explores a long-term research base inspired by the cold-climate environment of Hokkaido, Japan.

The concept investigates how architecture, environmental observation, renewable energy, forests, freshwater systems, and resilient infrastructure may coexist within a single research environment while respecting local culture and natural conditions.

- disaster resilience

- biodiversity

## Architectural Concept

The architectural language explores:

- Japanese simplicity
- natural timber
- large glazing
- deep roof overhangs
- passive environmental design
- harmony with nature
- resilient construction

The objective is to create a calm research environment capable of supporting long-term observation while remaining closely connected to the surrounding landscape.

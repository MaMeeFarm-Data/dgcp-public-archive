## Concept Statement

> Live within the environment.  
> Observe the system.  
> Preserve the evidence.

DGCP‚ĄĘ Research Base / Node Concept #0001 explores a tropical integrated systems research base within the environmental context of Chanthaburi, Thailand.

The concept brings together living, research, observation, documentation, environmental monitoring, and supporting infrastructure within a calm and nature-integrated environment.

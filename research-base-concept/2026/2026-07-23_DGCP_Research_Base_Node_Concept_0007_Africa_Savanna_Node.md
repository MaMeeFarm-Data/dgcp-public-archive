# DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0007

## Africa Savanna Node

### Giraffe Research Base

**Record ID:** RNC-000007

---

## Concept Statement

> Observe life.  
> Understand coexistence.  
> Respect the wild.  
> Build for the future.

DGCP™ Research Base / Node Concept #0007 explores a long-term research base inspired by the African savanna ecosystem of Kenya.

The concept investigates how architecture, wildlife conservation, ecosystem observation, environmental research, sustainable living, and community engagement may coexist within a single research environment while minimizing impact on surrounding habitats.

---

## Reference Environment

**Reference Location**

Laikipia Plateau, Kenya

Conceptual characteristics include:

- open savanna ecosystem
- wildlife migration corridors
- seasonal rivers
- acacia woodland
- warm daytime climate
- cool nights
- conservation landscapes
- protected natural habitats

---

## Research Role

Primary research themes include:

- wildlife observation
- savanna ecosystem research
- biodiversity monitoring
- human–wildlife coexistence
- conservation education
- sustainable living systems
- environmental monitoring
- long-term evidence preservation

---

## Architectural Concept

The architectural language emphasizes harmony between people and nature while minimizing environmental impact.

Design principles include:

- respect the land
- open connection with wildlife
- elevated low-impact structures
- passive ventilation
- local materials
- observation-focused design
- sustainable resource use

The architecture is designed to support research without disturbing the surrounding ecosystem.

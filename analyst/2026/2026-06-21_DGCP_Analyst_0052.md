### System Context

Networks remain among the most important yet least visible systems supporting modern civilization.

Information moves through networks.

Businesses operate through networks.

Governments communicate through networks.

Financial systems depend on networks.

Digital economies depend on networks.

Many observers focus on devices, applications, websites, or communication services.

The discussion frequently centers on what people use.

The systems enabling connectivity receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across telecommunications, internet infrastructure, cloud services, digital commerce, and global communications, I found myself paying less attention to devices and more attention to the infrastructure connecting them.

The connection remained visible.

The communication infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about technology alone.

The observation was about continuity.

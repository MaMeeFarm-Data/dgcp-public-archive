### System Context

Accountability remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Institutions depend on accountability.

Governance depends on accountability.

Trust depends on accountability.

Verification depends on accountability.

Civilization depends on accountable systems.

Many observers focus on decisions, leadership, regulations, or organizational performance.

The discussion frequently centers on individual actions.

The systems ensuring responsibility receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across governance systems, regulatory frameworks, public institutions, corporate management, and operational oversight, I found myself paying less attention to individual decisions and more attention to the infrastructure that assigns, records, and sustains responsibility.

The decision remained visible.

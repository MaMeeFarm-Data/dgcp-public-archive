### System Context

Improvement remains one of the most important yet least visible capabilities supporting modern civilization.

Infrastructure improves continuously.

Organizations improve continuously.

Institutions improve continuously.

Civilization advances through continuous improvement.

Many observers focus on major breakthroughs, innovations, or visible achievements.

The discussion frequently centers on significant milestones.

The incremental improvements supporting long-term progress receive less attention.

The achievement remained visible.

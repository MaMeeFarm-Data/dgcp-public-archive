### System Context

Knowledge remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Learning depends on knowledge.

Institutions depend on knowledge.

Innovation depends on knowledge.

Governance depends on knowledge.

Civilization depends on knowledge continuity.

Many observers focus on information, education, expertise, research, or intellectual achievements.

The discussion frequently centers on knowledge itself.

The systems enabled by knowledge receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across education systems, scientific research, governance structures, industrial development, and technological progress, I found myself paying less attention to knowledge as content and more attention to the infrastructure operating through it.

The knowledge remained visible.

The decision infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about learning alone.

The observation was about continuity.

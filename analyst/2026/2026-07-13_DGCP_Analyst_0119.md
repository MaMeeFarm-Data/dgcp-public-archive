### System Context

Sustainment remains one of the most important yet least visible capabilities supporting modern civilization.

Infrastructure depends on sustainment.

Organizations depend on sustainment.

Institutions depend on sustainment.

Civilization depends on the preservation of operational capability.

Many observers focus on creation, deployment, or initial success.

The discussion frequently centers on what systems can build or achieve.

The sustained capability required to keep those systems functioning over time receives less attention.

While reviewing developments across infrastructure, governance, healthcare, finance, logistics, manufacturing, scientific research, and digital systems, I found myself paying less attention to initial capability and more attention to the sustainment allowing interconnected systems to remain operational over time.

The operation remained visible.

The preserved operational capability became visible.

The observation was not about continuation alone.

The observation was about sustaining capability.

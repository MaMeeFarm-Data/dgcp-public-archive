### System Context

Transparency remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Institutions depend on transparency.

Governance depends on transparency.

Accountability depends on transparency.

Trust depends on transparency.

Civilization depends on transparent systems.

Many observers focus on reports, disclosures, regulations, or public information.

The discussion frequently centers on what is published.

The systems enabling visibility receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across public administration, corporate governance, financial reporting, scientific research, and digital information systems, I found myself paying less attention to published information and more attention to the infrastructure making information observable and understandable.

The report remained visible.

The transparency infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about disclosure alone.

The observation was about visibility.

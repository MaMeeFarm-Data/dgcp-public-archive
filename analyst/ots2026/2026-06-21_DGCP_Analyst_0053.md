### System Context

Information remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Decisions depend on information.

Organizations depend on information.

Governments depend on information.

Markets depend on information.

Civilization depends on information continuity.

Many observers focus on data, reports, news, databases, or digital content.

The discussion frequently centers on information itself.

The systems enabled by information receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across governance systems, financial markets, digital platforms, scientific research, and global communications, I found myself paying less attention to information as content and more attention to the infrastructure operating through it.

The data remained visible.

The information infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about knowledge alone.

The observation was about continuity.

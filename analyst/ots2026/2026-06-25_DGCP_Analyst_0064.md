### System Context

Coordination remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Organizations coordinate.

Institutions coordinate.

Infrastructure coordinates.

Markets coordinate.

Civilization coordinates.

Many observers focus on individual activities.

The discussion frequently centers on execution.

The systems organizing those activities receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across governments, infrastructure networks, logistics systems, financial systems, and digital platforms, I found myself paying less attention to individual operations and more attention to the infrastructure enabling them to function together.

The activity remained visible.

The coordination infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about management alone.

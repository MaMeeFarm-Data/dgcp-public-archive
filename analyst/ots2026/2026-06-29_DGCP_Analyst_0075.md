### System Context

Decisions remain one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Organizations depend on decisions.

Infrastructure depends on decisions.

Governance depends on decisions.

Communities depend on decisions.

Civilization depends on decision quality.

Many observers focus on outcomes, actions, policies, or leaders.

The discussion frequently centers on visible results.

The decision systems producing those results receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across governments, corporations, infrastructure planning, scientific research, and economic systems, I found myself paying less attention to outcomes and more attention to the infrastructure supporting informed decisions.

The outcome remained visible.

The decision infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about choices alone.

The observation was about continuity.

### System Context

Understanding remains one of the most important yet least visible capabilities supporting modern civilization.

Infrastructure depends on understanding.

Organizations depend on understanding.

Institutions depend on understanding.

Civilization depends on structural understanding.

Many observers focus on collecting information, measuring performance, or reporting events.

The discussion frequently centers on what is observable.

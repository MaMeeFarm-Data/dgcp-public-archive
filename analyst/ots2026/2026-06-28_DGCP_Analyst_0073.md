### System Context

Documentation remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Knowledge depends on documentation.

Operations depend on documentation.

Standards depend on documentation.

Governance depends on documentation.

Civilization depends on documented systems.

Many observers focus on reports, manuals, records, policies, or technical documents.

The discussion frequently centers on documents themselves.

The systems sustained through documentation receive less attention.

The document remained visible.

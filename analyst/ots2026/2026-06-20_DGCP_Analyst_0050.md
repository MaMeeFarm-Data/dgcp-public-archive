### System Context

Trust remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations of modern civilization.

Financial systems depend on trust.

Institutions depend on trust.

Markets depend on trust.

Communities depend on trust.

Civilization depends on trust.

Many observers focus on laws, contracts, transactions, organizations, or governments.

The discussion frequently centers on visible structures.

The systems enabling those structures to function receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across financial systems, governance systems, economic activity, institutions, and social organization, I found myself paying less attention to the structures themselves and more attention to the foundation supporting them.

The institution remained visible.

The trust infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about confidence alone.

The observation was about continuity.

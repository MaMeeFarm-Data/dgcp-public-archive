### System Context

Governance remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Institutions depend on governance.

Infrastructure depends on governance.

Markets depend on governance.

Communities depend on governance.

Civilization depends on governance continuity.

Many observers focus on governments, leaders, organizations, regulations, or public policy.

The discussion frequently centers on authority.

The systems coordinating authority receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across governments, corporations, international organizations, infrastructure systems, and public institutions, I found myself paying less attention to individual leaders and more attention to the infrastructure enabling coordinated decisions over time.

The institution remained visible.

The governance infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about authority alone.

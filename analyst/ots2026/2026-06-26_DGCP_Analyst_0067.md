### System Context

Maintenance remains one of the most important yet least visible foundations supporting modern civilization.

Infrastructure requires maintenance.

Equipment requires maintenance.

Buildings require maintenance.

Networks require maintenance.

Civilization depends on maintenance continuity.

Many observers focus on new construction, technological innovation, expansion, or growth.

The discussion frequently centers on creating new systems.

The systems preserving existing capability receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across transportation infrastructure, energy systems, manufacturing facilities, digital infrastructure, and public utilities, I found myself paying less attention to new assets and more attention to the infrastructure sustaining existing ones.

The asset remained visible.

The longevity infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about repair alone.

The observation was about preservation.

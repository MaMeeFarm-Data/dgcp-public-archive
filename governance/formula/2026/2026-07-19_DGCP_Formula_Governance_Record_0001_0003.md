## Purpose

This governance record documents a pre-OTS correction affecting DGCP™ Formula #0001, #0002, and #0003.

During the initial GitHub archive preparation, the visual reference images supplied for content interpretation did not correspond to the formula images identified by the associated IPFS references.

The IPFS references and formula sequence numbers were correct.

However, the initial filenames, titles, and explanatory content were created from incorrect visual references.

The mismatch was identified before the files were included in an OTS proof package.

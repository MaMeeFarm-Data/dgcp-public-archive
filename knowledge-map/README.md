# DGCP™ Knowledge Map Archive

A public archive of educational knowledge maps created under the DGCP™ (Data Governance & Continuous Proof) framework.

These visual knowledge maps simplify complex systems into easy-to-understand structures for public learning and system thinking.

---

## Purpose

The DGCP™ Knowledge Map series explores how real-world systems are structured and connected.

Topics may include:

- Companies
- Industries
- Infrastructure
- Transportation
- Supply Chains
- Technology
- Finance
- Countries
- Agriculture
- Energy
- Science
- Global Systems

The objective is to improve structural understanding through visual learning.

## Purpose

This educational knowledge map presents a structural overview of Google and illustrates how the company integrates search, advertising, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, software platforms, digital services, and global infrastructure into a connected technology ecosystem.

The objective is to simplify Google's overall business architecture into an accessible visual learning resource.

## Core Learning Areas

### Search Platform

Google operates one of the world's largest search platforms, providing access to web pages, images, videos, maps, news, and knowledge through large-scale indexing and information retrieval.

---

### Digital Services

Google provides a broad portfolio of digital services including:

- Search
- YouTube
- Gmail
- Google Workspace
- Chrome
- Google Play
- Google Maps

These services form a connected ecosystem supporting communication, productivity, navigation, and content discovery.

---

### Cloud & Infrastructure

Google Cloud provides enterprise cloud computing services including:

- Compute
- Storage
- Databases
- Networking
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning services

The platform supports organizations through scalable global infrastructure.

---

### Artificial Intelligence

Google develops artificial intelligence technologies including:

- Google AI
- Gemini
- Machine Learning
- DeepMind research
- Responsible AI initiatives

These technologies support products, research, and enterprise services.

---

### Data & Innovation

Google applies large-scale data analytics, research, and engineering through services including BigQuery, Looker, Data Studio, and Google Research to support continuous innovation.

---

### Customer Value

Google focuses on providing:

- Fast access to information
- Free and scalable digital tools
- Cloud services
- AI-powered capabilities
- Global accessibility
- Integrated user experiences

---

## Educational Perspective

This knowledge map demonstrates how Google combines search, cloud computing, digital platforms, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and global infrastructure into a connected technology ecosystem.

It emphasizes structural relationships between technology platforms, users, developers, partners, and digital services rather than commercial recommendations.

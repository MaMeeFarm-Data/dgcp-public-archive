# DGCP™ Knowledge Map #0001

## Agoda

### Understanding an Online Travel Booking Platform

---

## Purpose

This educational knowledge map presents a structural overview of Agoda and illustrates how an online travel platform connects travelers with accommodation providers, airlines, transportation services, and travel-related businesses through a digital marketplace.

The objective is to simplify the overall business ecosystem into an accessible visual learning resource.

---

## Knowledge Overview

The map explores:

- Company overview
- Mission and value proposition
- User booking workflow
- Supplier ecosystem
- Technology and data capabilities
- Revenue model
- Payment methods
- Customer benefits
- Overall business connectivity

---

## Core Learning Areas

### What is Agoda?

Agoda is an online travel booking platform focused primarily on Asia while serving travelers worldwide.

The platform is part of Booking Holdings and provides accommodation, flights, activities, and travel-related services.

---

### How the Platform Works

1. Search
2. Compare
3. Choose
4. Book
5. Confirm

The platform connects customer demand with travel suppliers through a digital booking system.

---

### Business Ecosystem

Agoda works with multiple travel partners, including:

- Hotels
- Resorts
- Apartments
- Villas
- Airlines
- Car rental companies
- Activity providers
- Insurance services
- Airport transfer providers

---

### Technology

The platform utilizes modern digital technologies including:

- Big Data
- Analytics
- Machine Learning
- Dynamic Pricing
- Fraud Detection
- Multi-language support
- Multi-currency services
- Mobile applications
- Web platform

---

### Revenue Model

Revenue is generated through multiple channels including:

- Booking commissions
- Service fees
- Advertising
- Promotional services
- Additional travel products

---

### Customer Value

The platform aims to provide:

- Competitive pricing
- Wide travel choices
- Fast online booking
- Verified reviews
- Continuous customer support

---

## Educational Perspective

This knowledge map demonstrates how digital platforms integrate customers, suppliers, payment systems, data, and technology into a connected travel ecosystem.

It highlights the structural relationships between participants rather than focusing on individual products or commercial recommendations.

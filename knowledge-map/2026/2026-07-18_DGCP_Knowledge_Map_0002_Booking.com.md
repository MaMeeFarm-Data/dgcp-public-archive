# DGCP™ Knowledge Map #0002

## Booking.com

### Understanding a Global Digital Travel Platform

---

## Purpose

This educational knowledge map presents a structural overview of Booking.com and illustrates how a global digital travel platform connects travelers with accommodation providers, transportation services, and travel-related partners through an integrated online marketplace.

The objective is to simplify the platform ecosystem into an accessible visual learning resource.

---

## Knowledge Overview

The map explores:

- Accommodation services
- Flight booking
- Car rental
- Airport taxi
- Global property network
- Traveler ecosystem
- Property partners
- Payment options
- Reviews and ratings
- Mobile application
- Personalization
- Revenue model
- Technology and data
- Trust and safety

---

## Core Learning Areas

### Global Accommodation Network

Booking.com provides access to a wide range of accommodations, including hotels, apartments, villas, resorts, hostels, guest houses, and unique stays across the world.

---

### Integrated Travel Services

The platform supports multiple travel services through one ecosystem, including:

- Accommodation
- Flights
- Car rental
- Airport transfers

These services help travelers organize complete journeys from a single platform.

---

### Digital Marketplace

The platform connects multiple participants, including:

- Travelers
- Property owners
- Property managers
- Hotels
- Hospitality partners
- Transportation providers

This creates a global digital marketplace linking travel demand with service providers.

---

### Technology

Booking.com utilizes digital technologies including:

- Big Data Analytics
- Machine Learning
- Dynamic Pricing Support
- Fraud Detection
- Cloud Infrastructure
- Real-time Availability

These technologies improve platform performance and user experience.

---

### Revenue Model

Revenue is generated through multiple channels including:

- Booking commissions
- Listings and promotions
- Sponsored placements
- Ancillary services
- Advertising partnerships

---

### Customer Value

The platform aims to provide:

- Wide accommodation choices
- Flexible travel planning
- Secure payment options
- Trusted guest reviews
- Personalized recommendations
- Global accessibility

---

## Educational Perspective

This knowledge map demonstrates how a digital travel platform integrates travelers, accommodation providers, transportation services, payment systems, customer reviews, and technology into a connected global travel ecosystem.

It emphasizes the structural relationships among participants rather than commercial recommendations.

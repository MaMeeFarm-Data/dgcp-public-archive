## Purpose

This educational knowledge map presents a structural overview of Amazon and illustrates how the company integrates e-commerce, cloud computing, logistics, digital services, technology, and global operations into one interconnected ecosystem.

The objective is to simplify the overall business architecture into an accessible visual learning resource.

---

## Knowledge Overview

The map explores:

- E-Commerce Store
- Marketplace
- Amazon Prime
- Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- Digital Content & Entertainment
- Logistics & Fulfillment
- Payment & Financial Services
- Devices & Hardware
- Technology & Innovation
- Data & Intelligence
- Sustainability
- Global Operations
- Customer Experience
- Revenue Streams

---

## Core Learning Areas

### E-Commerce Platform

Amazon operates one of the world's largest online retail platforms, providing millions of products through both direct retail operations and third-party marketplace sellers.

---

### Marketplace Ecosystem

The platform connects customers with independent sellers while providing fulfillment, logistics, analytics, and global expansion opportunities.

---

### Cloud Computing

Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides cloud infrastructure, computing services, storage, networking, artificial intelligence, and enterprise technology solutions used worldwide.

---

### Logistics Network

Amazon manages an extensive logistics ecosystem including fulfillment centers, inventory management, packaging, shipping, and customer returns to support large-scale operations.

---

### Technology & Data

Amazon applies technologies including:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Robotics
- Recommendation Systems
- Voice Technology
- Real-time Analytics
- Customer Insights
- Data-driven Decision Making

---

### Revenue Model

Revenue is generated through multiple business segments including:

- Online retail sales
- Third-party marketplace services
- AWS cloud services
- Amazon Prime subscriptions
- Digital advertising
- Digital content
- Consumer devices
- Other technology services

---

### Customer Value

Amazon focuses on providing:

- Large product selection
- Competitive pricing
- Fast delivery
- Reliable services
- Continuous innovation
- Customer-centric experiences

---

## Educational Perspective

This knowledge map demonstrates how Amazon combines commerce, cloud computing, logistics, technology, digital services, and data into a globally connected business ecosystem.

It emphasizes structural relationships between platforms, infrastructure, customers, partners, and technology rather than commercial recommendations.

# DGCP™ Philosophy

The DGCP™ Philosophy archive preserves the long-term principles, values, and reflections developed through continuous observation, evidence, and practical experience.

Unlike operational records or analytical reports, the Philosophy series documents the foundational ideas that guide the DGCP™ framework over time.

The archive is intended to remain stable, versioned, and publicly accessible as part of the DGCP™ knowledge infrastructure.

## Core Philosophy

DGCP™ views philosophy as something built through practice rather than declared through statements.

The framework follows a continuous progression:

Practice

↓

Observation

↓

Evidence

↓

Learning

↓

Principles

↓

Values

Values are expected to emerge through repeated practice, accumulated evidence, and long-term verification.

---

## Design Principles

- Observation before conclusion
- Evidence before belief
- Continuous learning
- Long-term thinking
- Structural consistency
- Public knowledge preservation

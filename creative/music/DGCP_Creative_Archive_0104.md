## Verse 1

A package waits upon a shelf

A simple object by itself

Most people see the final stage

Not the journey across the age

Fields and factories far away

Working night and working day

Roads and ports and cargo lines

Moving through the hands of time

## Pre-Chorus

We see the product in our hand

But not the systems that expand

Across the oceans, across the land

Connecting people through demand

## Chorus

The invisible supply chain

Moving through the sun and rain

Across the borders, seas, and skies

Hidden underneath our lives

The invisible supply chain

Every link must still remain

DGCP follows every trace

Connecting systems place to place

## Verse 2

A grain of wheat, a drop of oil

Machines that work and human toil

Warehouses and shipping routes

Networks built to carry through

Every truck and every train

Every vessel on the waves

Moving pieces one by one

Until the journey has been done

## Bridge

Food and water

Fuel and steel

Medicine and daily needs

Everything that people use

Travels paths they never view

Behind convenience stands a chain

Built through effort, risk, and strain

The world appears both near and small

Because this network moves it all

## Final Chorus

The invisible supply chain

Moving through the sun and rain

Across the borders, seas, and skies

Supporting everyday life

The invisible supply chain

The structure underneath the frame

DGCP records the flow

Helping future generations know

## Overview

Cybersecurity is more than firewalls.

It is the discipline of protecting systems, data, infrastructure, applications, and people through secure design, risk management, resilience, governance, and continuous improvement.

This public learning board introduces Cybersecurity from a systems thinking perspective, emphasizing relationships between technology, processes, people, threats, security architecture, and long-term operational resilience.

---

## Learning Topics

1. What is Cybersecurity?
2. Threat vs Risk vs Impact
3. Systems Thinking for Cybersecurity
4. Cybersecurity Life Cycle
5. High-Level Security Architecture
6. Good Security Systems
7. Common Trade-offs
8. Key Principles
9. Cybersecurity Mindset

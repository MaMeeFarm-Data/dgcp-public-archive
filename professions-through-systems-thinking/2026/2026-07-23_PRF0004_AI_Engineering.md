## Overview

AI Engineering is more than building models.

It is the discipline of designing, deploying, operating, and continuously improving intelligent systems that create reliable, responsible, and scalable value.

This public learning board introduces AI Engineering from a systems thinking perspective, emphasizing relationships between data, models, infrastructure, applications, governance, observability, and continuous learning.

---

## Learning Topics

1. What is AI Engineering?
2. AI vs Traditional Engineering
3. Systems Thinking for AI Engineering
4. AI Engineering Life Cycle
5. High-Level AI System Architecture
6. Good AI Systems
7. Common Trade-offs
8. Key Principles
9. AI Engineer Mindset

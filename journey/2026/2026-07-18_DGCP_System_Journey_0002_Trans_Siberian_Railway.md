## Purpose

This educational journey explores the Trans-Siberian Railway as one of the world's largest transportation systems.

Rather than focusing on tourism, the objective is to understand how geography, engineering, logistics, infrastructure, and economic development are connected across an entire continent.

## System Observation

The Trans-Siberian Railway is more than a transportation corridor.

It demonstrates how long-distance infrastructure connects industries, ports, cities, natural resources, and populations across multiple climates and time zones.

Large infrastructure systems influence trade, settlement, logistics, and regional development over generations.

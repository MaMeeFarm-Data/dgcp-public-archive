## Purpose

This educational journey explores one of the major overland connections between Southeast Asia and Eurasia.

Rather than focusing on tourism, the objective is to understand how geography, transportation, infrastructure, logistics, history, and economic systems connect regions across continents.

---

## Journey Overview

Bangkok, Thailand

**Destination**

Moscow, Russia

**Approximate Distance**

- Air: ~7,000 km
- Rail (via Trans-Siberian Railway): ~8,736 km

## System Observation

This journey demonstrates that transportation routes are more than methods of travel.

They represent connected systems that support trade, energy movement, tourism, cultural exchange, infrastructure investment, and long-term regional development.

Understanding these systems provides a broader perspective on how countries interact across geography.
